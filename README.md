Hello everyone! Merry Christmas 2025! This is a script of a fun christmas game in which we have to control a santa and collect as much gifts we can! Rules for this game are:

1. Press enter to start playing
2. Control and move santa with arrow keys
3. Try to collect as much gifts you can by moving santa to them
4. Every gift you miss, will minus your score
5. Every gift you collect will increase your score
6. Once you reach any hundred like 100, 200, 1100..., game will stop for 2 seconds displaying the score you reached and the speed for santa's movement will increase
7. Just make as much score you can and enjoy!

And now, here are the steps to play:

1. Download the folder "Python Christmas Project" from this repository.
2. Open the folder in your PC and run the Main.py file
3. Follow the rules and enjoy the game!
